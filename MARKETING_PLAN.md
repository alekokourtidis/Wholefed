# Wholefed Marketing Plan

Source of truth for Wholefed's launch and ongoing growth strategy.
Built on the Cal AI playbook (verified — 17yo founder, $1M MRR in 6 months, sold for $40M to MyFitnessPal).

---

## 1. Positioning (the wedge)

**Wholefed's unique edge:** personalized objective health tips based on YOUR specific health conditions, from a photo of any food (no barcode required).

**Vs. competitors:**
- **Yuka** (70M users): scans barcodes of packaged products only. Cannot scan restaurant meals, smoothies, salads, home-cooked food. Cannot personalize.
- **Cal AI** ($50M ARR): scans food for calories/macros only. No health-condition awareness.
- **MyFitnessPal**: manual logging, no AI scanning, generic.

**The hook:** *"Yuka and Cal AI tell EVERYONE the same thing. Wholefed tells YOU what's right for YOUR body — the same Sweetgreen bowl is different for a celiac vs. diabetic vs. someone with PCOS."*

The chronic-health-condition angle is the moat. Yuka can't personalize without rebuilding their product. Cal AI doesn't care about conditions.

## 4. Launch playbook (timeline)

### Phase 0 — Pre-approval prep (do NOW while waiting for Apple)

**Week 1 — Setup (3–4 hours)**
- Create 3 TikTok accounts with 3 different emails. Real-looking profile photos and bios.
- Warm up each account: scroll FYP for 5+ min/day on each, like 10 videos in your niche, follow 5 relevant accounts. Do this for 7 days before posting. (Skipping = shadowban risk.)
- Create matching @wholefed handles on Instagram and YouTube Shorts.

**Week 2 — Build creator list (4–5 hours)**
- 100 creators across 5 chronic-health niches (20 per niche)
- 30 min/day × 7 days
- Capture: handle, follower count, niche, recent post that resonates, email from bio/Linktree

**Week 3 — Batch shoot first content drop (Sunday, 4 hours)**
- 21 videos in one session: 7 talking head + 7 myths + 7 POV scans
- Don't post anything yet — videos sit in drafts until launch day

### Phase 1 — Launch day (the day Wholefed approves)

**Hour 0:**
- Post Video #1 on each of 3 accounts, staggered by 2 hours
- Email blast to 100 creators with launch announcement + offer
- Update alekotools.com to remove "App Store preview" and link to live App Store
- Post on alekotools blog: "Wholefed is live"

**Days 1–7:**
- Post 1–3 videos per account per day (3–9 total/day across 3 accounts)
- Reply to every comment on every video for first 72 hours (algorithm signal)
- Track which videos break 50k views — winning formats

### Phase 2 — Influencer outreach (continuous from Day 1)

- 30 outreach DMs/day, 5 days/week
- Test multiple scripts per niche
- Track what converts; kill what doesn't
- Top performers get rebooked or revenue share

### Phase 3 — Iterate on winners (Week 3+)

- Each Sunday: review top 3 videos of the week
- Identify common element (hook, food choice, voice, edit pattern)
- Next batch: 70% replicate winners, 30% new experiments
- Cut formats that fail after 5 attempts

### Phase 4 — Paid ads (Month 2 or 3, NOT before)

- Trigger: 5+ organic videos hit 100k+ views AND organic conversions are tracked
- Take top 3 organic videos → whitelist as TikTok Spark Ads + Meta whitelisted ads
- Start: $50/day. If CAC < LTV, scale to $200/day, then $500
- Don't try to fix bad creative with money. Money amplifies what works.
- **This is the StudyPebble lesson:** ads failed there because creative wasn't validated organically first. Cal AI's Phase 1 → Phase 2 sequencing is non-negotiable.

## 5. App Store optimization (do BEFORE approval)

- **Screenshots:** Show scan-to-result flow visually. First screenshot must communicate the app's job in 1 second.
- **Title/Subtitle:** "Wholefed: Scan Food, See Truth" or similar. Stuff with top keywords (food scanner, nutrition, health conditions).
- **Description lead:** *"Most health apps need a barcode. Wholefed scans any food — restaurant meals, salads, smoothies, home cooking — and personalizes the insight to YOUR health conditions."*
- **In-app review prompts:** Trigger after positive moments (3 successful scans, healthy verdicts). 5-star reviews directly increase ranking.
- **Keyword research:** Use AppFollow or App Annie (free tier).

## 7. Commitment rules (non-negotiable)

1. **3 months of daily posting** before judging if it's working. Cal AI posted 1,000+ videos. Most founders post 50 and quit.
2. **Sundays = batch day.** Block 3–4 hours every Sunday for shooting next week's content.
3. **30 outreach DMs/day**, 5 days/week.
4. **First $500 of revenue** goes back into creator payments, not pocket.
5. **No new app ideas for 90 days.** This is rule #1. The pattern of swapping ideas is what kills founders. Commit fully or don't start.
6. **No paid ads until Phase 4 trigger is hit** (5+ organic videos at 100k+ views).

## 10. What this plan is NOT

- Not a SaaS pitch. This is a consumer mobile app playbook.
- Not enterprise sales. No procurement, no compliance walls.
